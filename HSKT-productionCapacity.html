HSK Tangerang Total Production Capacity

At HSK Tangerang, our commitment to excellence and innovation is at the forefront of our operations. Our state-of-the-art facility exemplifies our dedication to delivering unmatched quality in the footwear industry. With advanced machinery and a team of skilled professionals, we have built a robust infrastructure that powers our expansive production capacity.

Every detail, from the number of machines to the daily output, is carefully calibrated to ensure that we not only meet but consistently exceed the expectations of our clientele. This detailed breakdown showcases the depth and breadth of our capabilities, highlighting our capacity to cater to diverse orders, be it bulk or bespoke.

Through persistent investments in technology and machinery, coupled with our staunch commitment to craftsmanship, we've established ourselves as a reliable ally in the footwear industry. The table below offers a detailed insight into our production capabilities across diverse categories, underscoring our dedication to precision, adaptability, and superior quality. While HSK Tangerang was the birthplace of our mass production endeavors, the majority of our machinery has since been transitioned to Central Java in alignment with our strategy to centralize mass production there. Presently, the focus of HSK Tangerang has shifted towards research and development, ensuring seamless operations at our Central Java production hub.
