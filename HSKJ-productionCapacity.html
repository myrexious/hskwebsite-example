HSK Jepara Total Production Capacity

The footwear industry, while rich in tradition, is also a realm of innovation and constant evolution. At PT Handal Sukses Jepara (HSK), we pride ourselves on being at the forefront of this dynamic landscape. While our foundation is rooted in the values of craftsmanship and quality, our approach is anything but traditional. We've embraced modern technologies and techniques to ensure that every piece of footwear that leaves our facility is a testament to our commitment to excellence.

The Central Java region, particularly Jepara, is fast emerging as the new hub for shoe manufacturing. Recognizing the potential, we ventured into this territory, armed with a vision and the drive to make a difference. While the initial days were marked by challenges, especially in terms of building a skilled workforce from scratch, it was our unwavering commitment and belief in our mission that saw us through. Today, our Jepara facility stands as a symbol of what can be achieved with the right mix of passion, vision, and perseverance.
