Product and Services

Delivering top-tier solutions, our products and services are crafted with precision, ensuring the utmost quality and reliability.

Injection Phylon EVA Midsole (IMEVA)

At the forefront of footwear innovation, our services specialize in the production of Injection Phylon EVA Midsoles (IMEVA). With an impressive fleet of 78 units of Injection EVA machines, we take pride in our state-of-the-art facilities that enable us to produce midsoles with precision and excellence. Boasting a staggering monthly output of 6,150,000 pairs, our capacity stands as a testament to our commitment to meeting the demands of an ever-growing industry.

Our prowess extends beyond mere numbers; we are renowned experts in Injection EVA, equipped with the skills to handle even the most intricate design models. Our team, composed of seasoned professionals, constantly strives for perfection, dedicating themselves to the continuous improvement and development of production techniques. Entrusting us with your needs means partnering with a team that prioritizes efficiency and quality in every endeavor.

Compression Phylon EVA Midsole (CMEVA)

Distinguished for our unparalleled proficiency in footwear technology, our services excel in the domain of Compression Phylon EVA Midsoles (CMEVA). Our advanced production facility houses an impressive collection of 40 units of CMEVA machines, empowering us to achieve a robust production capacity of 1,130,000 pairs. Notably, this establishes us as the footwear component supplier with the largest CMEVA capacity in Indonesia, underlining our dominant position in the industry.

In our relentless pursuit of excellence, we are constantly adapting and evolving. As a testament to our commitment to innovation and efficiency, we are currently transitioning to automatic machines. This strategic shift aims to amplify our productivity, ensuring that we continue to lead the market while delivering the best quality products to our clients.

EVA Compounding

Boasting a robust infrastructure in footwear technology, we proudly operate 10 state-of-the-art EVA compounding production lines, producing an impressive monthly output of 1,100 tonnes. Unlike many competitors, we have the distinct advantage of producing compounds specifically for our own production. This not only guarantees that every product adheres to the most rigorous specifications, but it also dramatically reduces potential lead times, given our independence from outsourced compounds.

Our commitment to excellence is unyielding. We are continuously engaged in research and development to uncover and refine superior EVA compound formulations, catering to the escalating demands for higher-grade specifications. Our dedication shines particularly in our pursuit of compounds that stand out in resilience and are remarkably lightweight, ensuring our clients receive nothing short of the best.

Coldshot - Direct Molded Press (DMP)

At the forefront of innovative footwear technology, we take immense pride in our Coldshot - Direct Molded Press (DMP) services, boasting a substantial capacity of 300,000 pairs per month. The uniqueness of the Coldshot - DMP process lies in its ability to produce dual-density midsoles or two-color midsoles. This distinctive capability often negates the necessity for midsole painting, offering a more streamlined and efficient production solution.

In line with our commitment to sustainability and efficiency, we are ambitiously venturing into a transformative project: substituting all diecut production models with DMP. This strategic shift not only elevates our production quality but also significantly curtails waste, reinforcing our dedication to environmentally conscious manufacturing and unparalleled quality.

Painting

Situated within our esteemed Jepara Facility, we operate five dedicated EVA painting lines, delivering an impressive monthly output of 300,000 pairs. Our adeptness in this sector is evident in our versatility, as we possess the capability to execute a diverse range of painting techniques tailored precisely to our customers' specifications and desires.

As we look ahead, our vision for the painting service remains clear and ambitious. We are unwavering in our commitment to sustainability, continually seeking innovative methods to refine our painting processes on midsoles. Collaborating closely with our valued partners, we aim to elevate design quality, ensuring that our offerings always remain at the cutting edge of industry standards.

CMP Blocker

Our facilities are well-equipped with a total of 6 CMP machines, ensuring that each location has access to this critical component of our production process. CMP machines are integral to our operations as they produce the prefoam blockers, the foundation upon which our high-quality compression EVA midsoles are built. The excellence of the end product begins with these blockers, emphasizing the importance of maintaining impeccable standards at this initial stage.

From the very outset, during the development phase, we place a heightened focus on designing our blockers with precision. Our primary aim is to preemptively address and mitigate potential issues that could arise during CMEVA production. This meticulous approach underscores our dedication to ensuring the highest quality in our compression EVA midsoles, right from the raw materials to the finished product.
