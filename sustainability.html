Sustainable Steps: The Footwear Future of a Greener Globe

Embracing the Eco-Revolution in Footwear.

Reducing Carbon Footprint (Solar Panel)

PT Handal Sukses Karya remains deeply committed to setting a benchmark in environmental stewardship within the footwear industry. In a revolutionary move aligning with our sustainability plans, we are excited to announce our partnership with PT. Solarion to establish state-of-the-art rooftop solar panels at our HSK Rembang and HSK Jepara facilities. With a robust capacity of 1,400 KWp at HSK Rembang and 1,100 KWp at HSK Jepara, these installations are more than just power sources; they symbolize our dedication to a greener future. To put the enormity of this project into perspective, the energy we're expected to harness will be equivalent to powering thousands of homes annually, significantly reducing our dependency on non-renewable energy sources.

Scheduled for completion by February 2024, this initiative is not just about energy savings but is a testament to our unwavering commitment to reduce the carbon footprint associated with midsole production. By integrating these solar solutions, we anticipate a substantial decrease in greenhouse gas emissions, illustrating our earnest efforts in driving sustainable innovation. As we march forward, we aspire to not just produce footwear, but to tread lightly on our planet, ensuring that each step taken is a stride towards a more sustainable future.

Bio-Based Material

Our commitment to sustainability extends beyond recycling and waste reduction. We are actively exploring the incorporation of bio-based materials into our compounds to create eco-friendly alternatives. One notable success is the adoption of bio-based materials such as Braskem, which are now in use for mass production at our facility. However, our journey towards sustainability is an ongoing one. We remain dedicated to research and development, continually striving to discover and implement new bio-based materials. Our ultimate aim is to pave the way for sustainable footwear that embodies our vision for a greener future.

Sustainability is woven into the fabric of our operations. We refuse to settle for the conventional and are determined to redefine what's possible in the realm of sustainable materials for the footwear industry.

EVA Recycling

Our EVA recycling facility, conveniently located in the Kudus area (just a 30-minute drive from our HSK Jepara facility), is equipped to process rejected parts and EVA waste, transforming them into eco-friendly recycled EVA sheets.

Why choose our recycled EVA sheets? Efficient Use: Our recycled EVA sheets serve as a valuable filler in virgin EVA compounds, optimizing product performance. Consistent Quality: Our recycling process ensures that recycled materials are uniformly distributed within the compound, guaranteeing quality. Environmentally Friendly: Our commitment to sustainability is evident through our dust-free and environmentally conscious recycling process.

Currently, we operate a single production line with a total capacity of 110 tons per month, ensuring a steady supply of recycled EVA sheets to meet your needs. We are dedicated to sustainability and actively work towards reducing and reusing the waste we generate, aligning with our mission for a greener tomorrow.
